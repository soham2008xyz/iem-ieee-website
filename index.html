  IEM IEEE STUDENT CHAPTER  


Institute of Engineering & Management has entered the prestigious club of institutions having its own IEEE Student Chapter. We're a group of electrical engineering and computer science students (undergraduate and graduate) who want to have fun and meet new people, to learn about research and innovation in technology, to find ourselves and our passions, to help our community, and to get inspired to change the world!

As the world's largest technical association, IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is comprised of a variety of groups, active in publications, conferences, and building technical communities. These units involve member outreach at both the local and global level.

"A Student Branch Chapter is a technical sub-unit of a Student Branch constituted by a minimum of six (6) IEEE members of Student Member or Graduate Student Member grade of a Society and established by petition to the parent Student Branch and Society concerned to represent and fulfill the needs of the members and the missions of IEEE. A Student Branch Chapter functions in a manner similar to a committee of the Student Branch."
as defined in IEEE Protocol

Teacher Councilor

Prof. Himadri Nath Saha

Himadri Nath Saha has completed his Bachelor Degree from Jadavpur University and Post Graduate Degree from Indian Institute of Engineering, Science and Technology (formerly, Bengal Engineering and Science University).
